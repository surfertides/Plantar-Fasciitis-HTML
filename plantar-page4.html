My Plantar Plan

 My Plantar Plan

Areas to focus on most

  1. Getting good-fitting shoes that will support my arch
  2. Watching my weight and making sure I don't put extra lbs on my feet (a.k.a. eating healthy)
  3. Doing daily stretches and activites that roll out my calf and leg muscles
  4. Biking and light exercises such as HIIT and yoga
  5. Resting your feet as much as possible and icing them morning and night
  6. MAKING SURE YOU DO THESE THINGS CONSISTANTLY AND WITHOUT FAIL!!!
